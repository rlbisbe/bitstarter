Traveling around the world can be a very enriching experience, so our goal is to provide a service that helps to get more from each trip. With trilo, yo will be able to place in a map your visits, put comments, and give advice about specific places. Using your facebook account, you will also be able to share those visits with friends, see your friends trips, and ask them for advice. The other great feature that is included is the places wishlist, that helps us to track where do we want to go and see where do our friends want to travel too. We could even organize a group trip!

This product can provide specific targeted advertisement related to the visited city and the places wishlist. This way if you are displaying a friend's page about Rome, you may have advertising or recommendations from booking.com for the hotel or lastminute.com for the flight. You may also put recommendations for places to eat or have a drink. This way local commerce can be shown side by side with user recommendations.

FAQ

How much it will cost?
It will be free for standard user. We are designing a Premium mode with more features and advertising limitations.
How much will it cost for advertisers?
We are talking with local companies and global advertising firms, price will depend on the city and the relevance.
Will be my trip information public?
Just if you want to, we are setting the default level to friends view, you can make a public profile (no login required to view your profile) or you can do a private profile (only available to friends).
